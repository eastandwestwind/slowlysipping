If I were to live out of a backpack, here are the items I would pack:

- laptop
- camera
- blanket
- light scarf
- hiking shoes
- slip on shoes
- warm puffy (but packable) jacket
- shell jacket
- compact travel hammock
- wool bra
- 1 pair jeans
- 1 pair comfy pants
- 1 flannel button-up
- couple of t-shirts
- 2 tank tops
- lightweight dress
- 1 pair shorts
